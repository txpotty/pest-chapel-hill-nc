Hearing scratching, scurrying, or chirping sounds coming from your attic, walls, or crawl space is a strong indicator of wildlife activity. Squirrels, raccoons, and bats are particularly notorious for making these noises as they move around, build nests, or forage for food. These sounds are not just unsettling; they can signify the presence of animals causing damage to your insulation, wiring, and ductwork. Professional wildlife control is essential to identify the source of the noises, remove the animals safely, and prevent them from returning.
